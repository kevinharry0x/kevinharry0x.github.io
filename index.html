Origin Story

The Awakening

My cyber journey truly began in my first year of college. I had always been curious about hacking, but I didn't know where to start. That's when I met Hemanth Karal Varmaa. I asked him for advice, and over time we became close friends — he became my mentor and teacher. He introduced me to his circle, which included seniors already deep in the field.

The Mentors

I became interested in bug bounty and met Malathi and Rishyendra — seniors who helped me learn new things and became like family. Hemanth helped me land an internship at IIT — where everything changed. There I met Kabil Preetham and Amithab: two extraordinary minds. Kabil speaks without ego. Amithab's presence still pushes me to learn more deeply.

The Eternal Student

If I hadn't met them, my life would have been a looping day without direction. My knowledge is still far from enough, but I will never stop learning. The people you meet shape your path. Grateful to every mentor who guided me.
